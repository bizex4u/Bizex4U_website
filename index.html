How It Works

From warehouse to hoarding
in 4 steps.

A simple, documented process built for enterprise procurement and marketing teams.

1

Agreement

We formalize the barter arrangement via a contract that maps your inventory to an agreed media value — no surprises.

2

Selection

Review our network of 500+ media options with rate cards. Choose the placements that match your campaign objectives and geography.

3

Exchange

A purchase order is issued for your inventory. Media vendors receive goods; your campaign is scheduled and confirmed.

4

Execution

Campaign launches with full documentation — photos, location reports, and performance metrics delivered directly to you.
